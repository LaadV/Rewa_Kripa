Common questions and how to get in touch with us

How do I book a seat?

Go to our Book Seat page, select your route, date, bus, and seat number. Enter your name and phone number. Click "Confirm on WhatsApp" — your booking details will be pre-filled. Just press Send on WhatsApp. We confirm within minutes.

Can I book on redBus, MakeMyTrip or Goibibo?

Yes! Rewa Kripa Travels is listed on redBus, Goibibo, MakeMyTrip, and AbhiBus. Or book directly with us on WhatsApp for fastest service.

What amenities do your buses have?

Reading Light, USB Charging, CCTV, Leg Rest, and M-Ticket support on all buses. Premium coaches also have WiFi, entertainment, and AC recliners.

What is the cancellation policy?

90% refund if cancelled 48+ hours before departure. 50% for 24–48 hours. No refund under 24 hours. See our full Cancellation Policy.

What ID should I carry?

Any valid government-issued photo ID — Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN, Passport, or Driving Licence.
