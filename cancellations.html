Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026

This policy complies with the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (India) and is binding on all bookings made with Rewa Kripa Travels, Bus Station, Navalpura, Barwani — 451551, MP.

1. Regular Seat / Route Ticket Cancellations

  • More than 48 hours before departure: 90% refund.
  • 24–48 hours before departure: 50% refund.
  • Less than 24 hours before departure: No refund. Ticket may be rescheduled once without charge (subject to availability).
  • No-show: No refund.

2. Special Trip / Package Tour Cancellations

  • 15+ days before departure: 75% refund of amount paid.
  • 8–14 days before: 50% refund.
  • 3–7 days before: 25% refund.
  • Less than 72 hours: No refund. Booking may be transferred to another person (with ID verification).

3. How to Request Cancellation

All cancellation requests must be made via WhatsApp at +91 99778 55338 or email at rewakripa@gmail.com. Written confirmation is mandatory. Verbal cancellations are not accepted.

4. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed within 7 working days of approval, to the original payment method. The Company is not liable for delays caused by banking institutions.

5. Cancellation by the Company

If the Company cancels a trip (natural calamity, government order, force majeure, vehicle breakdown), passengers receive a full refund or free rescheduling at their choice.

6. Rescheduling

One free rescheduling permitted up to 6 hours before departure. Subsequent rescheduling: ₹50 per seat fee applies.

For disputes, applicable law is the law of India. Jurisdiction: Courts of Barwani, Madhya Pradesh.
