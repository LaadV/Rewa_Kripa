About Rewa Kripa Travels

15+ years of connecting communities across Madhya Pradesh with safe, reliable travel

Our Story

Rewa Kripa Travels was founded in 2008 with a single bus and a simple mission — to make safe, comfortable, and affordable bus travel accessible to the people of Barwani and surrounding regions of Madhya Pradesh.

What began as a humble operation on the Barwani–Indore corridor has grown into one of the most trusted bus services in the region. Today, we operate 4 premium buses across routes connecting Barwani, Pati, Bokrata, Indore, and Anjad — serving thousands of passengers every month.

We are listed on redBus, Goibibo, MakeMyTrip, and AbhiBus — a testament to the trust passengers and platforms place in us. Our Justdial rating of 4.0 reflects the consistent quality of service our passengers experience.

Our office is located at Bus Station, Navalpura, Barwani — 451551, Madhya Pradesh. We serve commuters, students, pilgrims, and leisure travellers alike.
